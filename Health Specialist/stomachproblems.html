STOMACH PROBLEMS

It is a disorder of digestive function characterized by discomfort or heartburn or nausea.
Abdominal pain has many causes, some more serious than others.
What feels like a stomachache may be coming from another organ in your abdomen, or from outside of your digestive system.
Always seek medical care if your abdominal pain is unexplained, persistent or severe.
There are four types of Abdomenal pains:
			Right upper quadrant.
			Left upper quadrant.
			Right lower quadrant.
			Left lower quadrant.

CAUSES OF STOMACH PROBLEMS: 

1. Irritable bowel syndrome.
2.Functional dyspepsia.
3.Inflammatory bowel disease (Crohn's, ulcerative colitis).
4/Large or small bowel obstruction.
5.Small intestine cancer.
6.Colon cancer.
7.Abdominal aortic aneurysm.
8.Peritonitis.
9.Frequent discomfort, gas, bloating, constipation, diarrhea, and heartburn could be signs that your gut is having a hard time processing food and eliminating waste.

SYMPTOMS: 

1.The first sign of problems in the digestive tract often includes one or more of the following symptoms:
			Bleeding.
			Bloating.
			Constipation.
			Diarrhea.
			Heartburn.
			Incontinence.
			Nausea and vomiting.
			Pain in the belly.
2.Swallowing problems.
3.Weight gain or loss.

PREVENTION: 

1.Eat small, frequent meals. Prevent indigestion, bloating and heartburn by changing how often you eat without increasing your overall calorie intake.
2.Drink plenty of water and limit alcohol.
3.Exercise regularly. 
4.Maintain a healthy body weight.
5.Eat a balanced diet.
6.Manage your stress level.
7.Make fibre a major portion of your diet.
8.Incorporate both soluble and insoluble fibre.
9. Consider a probiotic.
10.Be active and exercise regularly.
11.Avoid or stop bad habits.
