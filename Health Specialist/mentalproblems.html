MENTAL PROBLEMS

INTRODUCTION: 
A mental disorder is characterized by a clinically significiant disturbance in an
individuals cognition ,emotional regulation ,or behaviour

CAUSES: 
1.Social isolation or loneliness.
2.Social disadvantage ,poverty,or debts.
3.Severe or long term stress.
4.Having a longterm physical health conditions
5.Bereavement(losing someone close to you).

SYMPTOMS: 
1.Feeling sad or down.
2.Confused thinking or reduced ability to concentrate.
3.EXtreme mood changes of highs and lows
4.Suicidal thinking.
5.Excessive anger.

PREVENTION: 
1.Being physically active.
2.Exercise can reduce feeling of stress and depression.
3.Getting enough sleep because sleep affects your mood.
4.healthy Eating.

DIET: 
1.Eating plenty of fruits and vegetables along with foods rich in omega-three fatty acids.
