What is health?

“Health is a state of complete physical, mental, and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity.”

Your health is at the center of your life. Every part of your life relies on you having good health.
You cannot climb higher in all the other seven areas of your life if you do not have enough physical energy.

Here we are giving some good food habits:

  • Load your plate with vegetables.
  • Eat a balanced breakfast.
  • Don't starve yourself.
  • Ask questions when you eat out.
  • Have a plan when you hit the grocery store.
  • Cut down on processed foods.
  • Limit your sodium and sugar.
  • Don't just count calories.
  • Enjoy your Food.
