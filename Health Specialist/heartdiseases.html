HEART PROBLEMS

The term “heart disease” refers to several types of heart conditions. The most common type of heart disease in the United States is coronary artery disease (CAD), which affects the blood flow to the heart. Decreased blood flow can cause a heart attack.

CAUSES OF HEART PROBLEMS: 

Leading risk factors for Heart diseases and strokes are:

1.High Blood pressure

2.High Low Density lipopten (LDL) cholestrol.
3. Diabetes
4.Smoking and second hand smoke exposure.
5.Obesity (weight gain).

SYMPTOMS: 

1.Chest pain,Chest tightness and chest presssure.
2.Shortness of breath.
3. Pain in the Neck,Jaw,Throat.
4.Pain,Numbness,Weakness, or coldness in the Legs or Arms.

PREVENTION: 

1.Give up smoking.
2. Reduce your alcohol consumption.
3.Keep your diabetes under control.
4. Maintain Healthy Weight.

DIET: 

The Items that will help you are:
1.Eat more low sodium-canned vegetables and fruits.
2.Whole Grains
3.Nuts
4.Fish and Poultry
5.Vegetable oils
6.Refined Carbohydrates
